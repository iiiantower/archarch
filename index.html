Oakwood Residence

Oakwood Residence is a serviced apartment that thoughtfully blends the comfort of home with the refinement of hotel living. Designed with attention to proportion, materials, and natural light, the spaces offer a calm and balanced atmosphere suited for both short stays and extended living.

Privacy, functionality, and attentive services come together to support a lifestyle that feels effortless yet well-considered. Whether for business or everyday living, Oakwood Residence provides a place where one can settle in, slow down, and feel at ease within the city.
